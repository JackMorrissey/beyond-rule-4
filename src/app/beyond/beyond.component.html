Financial Independence

Financial Independence is the idea that if you can save up enough money, eventually the earnings on those investments can cover your expenses and "work" becomes optional.

Read more at YNAB: Is the FIRE movement for you?

One of the obstacles with FI is that it's a marathon, not a sprint. It might be tough to stay motivated if you can't visualize how the small choices you make today vastly benefit the future.

So... what if you could foreshadow the future based on the actions of today?
